African Fashion Week
Middle East

African Fashion Week Middle East aims to bring about opportunity and connections within the African Middle East market. We aspire to create a platform for designers, show case their work and connect them to new markets and move away from stereotypical aesthetics.

As Africa fashion week Middle East, we look to promote designers with either African heritage or connected to the continent. We seek the unique, acentric, untold stories and bring awareness to the vast creatives of Africa. We aim to add value to designersâ€™ journey and empower them to flourish in our respective industry.
