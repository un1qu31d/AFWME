NEETA LULLA

Neeta Lulla, the 4-time National Award Winning Fashion Designer is the driving force behind our Fashion Design Department. She has created costumes for famous Bollywood movies like, Devdas, has facilitated for the entire apparel for the epic movie, Jodha Akbar and many other bespoke national as well as international projects. Being associated with Hamstech’s Fashion Design Department as a Celebrity Mentor, she enriches the lives of students through her experience and expertise from the fashion industry. It is under her guidance and mentoring that Hamstech transforms thousands of beginners into successful designers and international entrepreneurs.

DIVYA REDDY

Divya Reddy is a young and dynamic fashion designer who made her mark at the Lakme Fashion Week. Having graduated from The Illinois Institute of Art and New York Fashion Institute, she achieved a new pinnacle of fame in India's Fashion Industry within no time. As an advisory board member, she helps students learn from her belief that creating a style statement is all about balancing the creativity, art and science of designing. She also supports the ideology: “fashion is lot more than just blindly following current trends”.
